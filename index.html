PROBLEM STATEMENT :- Implementing a virtual lab to solve set of quadratic equations having one variable but different module and those are relative prime .

AIM :- Create a virtual lab to implement Chinese Remainder Theorem

THEORY :- The Chinese Remainder Theorem (CRT) is a technique to reduce modular calculations with large moduli to similar calculations for each of the (mutually co-prime) factors of the modulus. The first description of the CRT is by the Chinese mathematician Sun Zhu in the third century AD. The CRT makes it possible to reduce modular calculations with large moduli to similar calculations for each of the factors of the modulus. At the end, the outcomes of the subcalculations need to be pasted together to obtain the final answer. The big advantage is immediate: almost all these calculations involve much smaller numbers.
